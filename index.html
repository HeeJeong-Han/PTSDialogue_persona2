PTSDialogue

A conversational agent to sustain patient engagement in PTSD

Instruction

The is a prototype of a conversational agent for PTSD patients with the second persona. The second persona is friendly.
The goals of the project are 1) to develop a conversational agent that can deliver therapeutic content and provide supportive accountability in PTSD and 2) to assess feasibility and acceptability of the developed conversational agent for sustaining engagement with patients with PTSD.


To get started, click "Welcome to PTSDialogue!" to start the chatbot in the right below. If you cannot see it, please click orange box.


If you want to go back to the first dialogue, please chat "welcome".
